I did the [spotify year in review](https://www.spotify.com/us/wrapped). And I wasn't sure how to interpret the results, for one, my #1 artist was Fairuz, but that's not fair to other artists. Fairuz is a given, like she shouldn't even be competing honestly haha. Anyways, I took a few screenshots (sorry my phone is french haha). They basically say that I've listened to a (whole) lot of music and a lot of new things. I was comparing results with friends and was shocked that the majority of them didn't have stats like these (not that these numbers are any indication of taste or anything like that).

I just think that I love to try and diversify my sources of information and how I perceive the world. And that includes entertainment and in this case music. And also I hate algorithmic recommendations and try my best to stay away from them. This is a quick thought, I'm not arguing algorithm are bad, I just don't like them.

Actually I think I'm just posting this looking for an excuse to post that last screenshot somewhere, merci spotify!! it's literally one of the highlights of 2020 for me haha
